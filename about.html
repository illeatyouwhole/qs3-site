MISSION STATEMENT

Our mission is to bring the right expertise to bear on problems facing our clients, and by working together, develop innovative solutions to difficult questions.


PURPOSE, VALUES, & CODE OF CONDUCT

OUR PURPOSE: Quality Scientific Solutions will provide the best scientific advice to our clients by accessing the right group of experts needed to develop value adding, sustainable solutions to complex health, safety and environmental issues.

OUR VALUES: QS3 will deliver the best scientific solutions with integrity, scientific rigor, independence & discretion.

ORGANIZATIONAL PRINCIPLES

Quality Scientific Solutions, LLC. is a research and consulting company dedicated to helping clients create, maintain and defend products with the highest standard of safety and utility.

QS3 is a virtual company comprised of principals, associates and affiliates who have decades of experience in health and environmental sciences and regulatory affairs.

QS3, through its networks in academia, industry and government, has access to experienced health and environmental safety experts and regulatory affairs professionals.
