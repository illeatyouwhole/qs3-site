Purpose, Pillars, &
Code of Conduct

PURPOSE: Quality Scientific Solutions will provide the best scientific advice to our clients by accessing the right group of experts needed to develop value-adding, sustainable solutions to complex health, safety, and environmental issues.

ORGANIZATIONAL PILLARS: QS3 will deliver the best scientific solutions with integrity, scientific rigor, independence, and discretion.

CODE OF CONDUCT: QS3 will insure that the highest ethical and scientific standards are maintained in all of our work.


Mission

QS3 is a research consluting company dedicated to helping clients create products that meet the highest standard of safety and utility.

QS3 is a virtual company compromised of principals, associates, and affiliates who have decades of experience in neuroendocrinology, toxicology, pahology, reproductive and developmental biology, biostatistics, epidemiology, and environmental sciences.

QS3, through its networks in academia, industry, and government, has access to experienced health, safety, environmental, and regulatory professionals.

Our mission is to bring the right expertise to bear on problems facing our clients, and by working together, develop innovative solutions to difficult problems.
